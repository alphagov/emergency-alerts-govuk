How to opt out of emergency alerts

If you opt out, you will not get any emergency alerts.

You cannot choose the type of hazard you want to get alerts about. If you opt out because you do not want flood warnings for example, you’ll also miss alerts for fires and terrorist incidents.

Because of this, we recommend you keep Emergency Alerts switched on for your own safety.

Android phones and tablets

To opt out, search your settings for ‘emergency alerts’ and turn off Severe threats.

If this does not work, contact your device manufacturer.

iPhone and iPad

To opt out, search your settings for ‘emergency alerts’ and turn off Severe alerts.

If this does not work, contact your device manufacturer.
