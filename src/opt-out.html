How to opt out of emergency alerts

You can opt out of some emergency alerts, but not the most important ones.

You cannot opt out by subject, only by how serious the emergency is. If you opt out because you do not want flood warnings, for example, you might miss alerts for fires and terrorism.

Because of this, you should keep emergency alerts switched on for your own safety.

Android phones and tablets

To opt out, search your settings for ‘emergency alerts’ and turn off Severe alerts.

If this does not work, contact your device manufacturer.

Mobile phone network tests

Mobile network operators in the UK are testing emergency alerts.

You may get an alert if you have an Android phone or tablet.

To opt out of these test alerts:

  • Open your phone calling app.
  • Use the keypad to enter *#*#2627#*#*
  • Search your settings for ‘emergency alerts’ and turn off Test alerts.

iPhone

To opt out, search your settings for ‘emergency alerts’ and turn off Severe alerts.

If this does not work, contact your device manufacturer.
