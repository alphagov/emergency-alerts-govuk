Local emergency alerts

What are emergency alerts?

Alerts are a new way for the emergency services to warn people when lives are in danger.

They tell you what you need to do to stay safe.

In an emergency, every mobile phone and tablet in the affected area will get an alert.

How do they work?

Emergency alerts are sent using mobile phone masts. You get them based on where you are, not who you are.

Most people will get alerts automatically. You do not need to sign up.

The emergency services do not need your phone number to send you an alert.
